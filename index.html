Who are we

We are Witch Production, Film & TV Project production company in Georgia, Which Provides a whole service of pre-production, production and post production.

What We Do

We work with Film & TV Productions from all over the world. We are providing each and every client with premium quality services, from concept to delivery, for projects of any style in Georgia: Feature & Documentary films, TV Shows, Ads, Music Videos, Photo Shoot etc... Preproduction Service: Location Scouting, Travel and Itinerary, Permits, Fees, Insurance, Talent and Crew Booking, Budgeting Project Planning, Consulting, Script Research and Development, Storyboarding Production Service: Field and Studio Producing, Directing. Cinematography. Sound Production. Lighting Design. Camera Crews. Audio and Video Equipment. Set Design and Construction. Props. Costumes, Makeup and Hair.
