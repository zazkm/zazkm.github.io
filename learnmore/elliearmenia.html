Ellie Op Patrouille

“Ellie on Patrol” Dutch TV documentary series. This program is a documentary series made for Dutch national television network NPO 1 Police officer Ellie Lust exchanges the familiar streets of Amsterdam for those of other countries. She goes on patrol with her international police colleagues; not as a spectator, but really as part of the team.
Locations: Georgia and Armenia
