3 Op Reis

"3op Reis" Dutch Travel Show Filmed in Georgia. Chris continues his road trip through the Caucasus, an area of extremes. This week he will cross the border between Azerbaijan and Georgia . He makes a stop in the modern capital Tbilisi. Then he drives into the mountains. Chris continues his road trip through the Caucasus and arrives in Batumi, also known as the "Pearl on the Black Sea" Chris continues his road trip through the Caucasus, a region of extremes: one moment you are in a hypermodern city, the next in a medieval village. Chris drives to that last spot: Ushguli, the most remote village in all of Georgia . At least if his Lada makes it.
